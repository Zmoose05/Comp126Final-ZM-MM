About Our Company

360 EHAP, Inc. was founded by Jeff McKeel and Ray Hardee in July 2015. With over 25 years of experience, we strive to provide quality heating, air conditioning, plumbing, and electrical services at affordable prices to residents of Eastern North Carolina.

Our main office is located in Ayden, NC, but our service area includes Greenville, NC, New Bern, NC, Snow Hill, NC, Washington, NC, Williamston, NC, and the surrounding areas. We pride ourselves in contributing our best quality work to each jobsite.
