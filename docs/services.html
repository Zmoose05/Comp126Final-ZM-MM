Services

Here Are Some Of Our Available Services!

Service: Heating Systems

Winters in Eastern North Carolina tend to be mild, but there are some chilly days where you really need the heat pumping! We offer installs of heat exchangers, furnaces, heat pumps, and gas packs. We also repair heating units as the parts age, such as motors and coils. We service both residential and commercial units.

Service: Air Conditioning

Living in North Carolina, air conditioning is needed for a large portion of the year to keep your home cool! Some of our air conditioning services include unit installations and change-outs, preventative maintenance, duct installs and repairs, minor repairs to units, and service contracts for all brands of units. We service both residential and commercial units.

Click here to set up an appointment!

Service: Plumbing

One of our most common plumbing calls is for a water heater that is leaking or working improperly. We offer repairs and installations of both tankless and regular tank water heaters, as well as faucet repair and/or replacement, removing blockages from sewer lines, and the installation of new drain lines.

Click here to set up an appointment!

Service: Electrical

Currently, we only offer minor electrical repairs. We are looking to expand this in the coming years! Our current available offerings include: Replacing fixtures, swap and replace outlets, and installing outdoor low-voltage lighting.
